LensMarvel

A web portal for all level photographers

Problem Statement

There are many websites to learn photography online, upload photo online and find meetups. But none where everything related to photography can be find at one place. Usually after learning basics of photography user needs to uploads his photos, find for meetups where he can get more exposure and he will be googling and go on different websites for his work. The idea is to provide one portal where all level photographers needs are met and interaction with each other is possible.

Outcome Vision

  • Provide free tutorials for photoenthusiast
  • Expert level photographer arrange workshops and host onsite.
  • Facility to upload share pictures and get feedback.

Minimum Viable product

LENSMARVEL needs would be a responsive web portal dedicated to all level photographers from enthusiast to expert level photographers. An app can be considered to be design in later stages.Newbie can find text tutorial learn more as well find upcoming workshops nearby and book for it. A pro photographer can host their workshops on portal and can reach to maximum photo enthusiast and can get many more signups for workshops.

  • Video and text tutorial
  • Upload photos
  • Explore
  • Host Workshop and meetups
  • Search for meetups
