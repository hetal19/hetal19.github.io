Smart phone app to reserve parking before you reach destionation

Concern if you will get parking spot fast in vicinity of destination?

Problem Statement

Finding a parking slot is a well-known urban headache. City dwellers makes their sweet plan to visit a market place, watch a movie, attend a function, etc. But, hassles of getting a proper parking spoil their whole plan right at the beginning.

Challenge

Design smart phone app for users to book a parking slot in parking space in vicinity of his destination with his app depending on

  • Prefered location, Cost, automobile size
  • Proper and logical payment mechanism
  • Should be able to book while driving, hence very less interactions

Assumptions

  • Users of app are tech savvy
  • App owners have collaborated with the traffic police to solve the parking problem in the city and reserved parking spots in certain locations

User Research

Users in age group of 22-50years owning vehicle, driving often for work like shopping, meeting clients, visiting doctor any many more.

Research Question

  • How often do you go out in a week and need to find a parking space?
  • Minimum how many hours you need to park your vehicle when doing your chores ?
  • What are your major activities when you need park your vehicle. Eg: shopping, grocery buying, meeting client, lunch, visiting doctor?
  • When would you be booking your parking spot:
    • While driving
    • Before leaving from home
  • What is preferred mode of payment:
    • debit card
    • paytm
    • Cash

Research Findings

  • Atleast thrice a week I have to attend cient lunch meeting. Everytime I have to search for location to park and lot of time just goes intto it.
  • I should be able to choose my spot as per my automobile size, cost and location
  • Would reserve parking space before leaving ,hence save time
  • Wallet payment are faster and need not worry about the change

key takeaways

  • Choose parking spot as per desired location, automobile size and cost.
  • Should be able to do booking on the go hence very less interaction.
  • Easy payment method which does not involve too much typing.
  • Should be able to view location pictures.

User flow

Sketches

On entering area name, app shows map of that locality and available parking spots along with parking rates. User can view list also by clicking on list button on top right

When user clicks on any one location, its shows the details like pictures and a button to reserve spot appears.

Once user reserves the spot, he receives the receipt in form of barcode which he could use to park the vehicle.

Wireframes

Since the user is always in hurry while leaving for somewhere, hence I designed app consisting only five screens. User can book parking space on the go without much hassle. User should enter area name or area code on opening app. The app will show available free parking space along with rates. When user selects one spot, it shows details about that spot, after which user can reserve and select payment method. On reserving spot user gets a barcode receipt for confirmation.
